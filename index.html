ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm George and I love Web Development. I'm a Frontend Web Designer from Nigeria with a focus in user experience, visual and interactive design. I'm hoping to further my career into becoming a Full Stack Web Developer. I have been a designer since my University days mostly using Adobe PhotoShop and Coreldraw. Simply put I love designs. I now have a passion for creating websites that are captivating, fresh and inviting for users to spend time on.


My Journey into Web Development started in January 2021 when I started taking courses on FreeCodeCamp. These past few months has been incredible so far as I learn more on good design practise, creating responsive websites and working on personal projects. It has only been a steady growth in my path and I can say I have fallen in love already with programming. Each challenges I faced working on a project has only made me strived to learn more and I seek to apply what I leart from those challenges into each design I make.


I'm an Electrical and Electronic graduate from the University of Benin, I'm a football fan and I have a great social life. I'm open to collaborating and would love to learn more from others. Reach out to me if you need any work to be done!
